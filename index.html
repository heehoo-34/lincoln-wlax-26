ABOUT US

Lincoln Women's Lacrosse was established in 2023 by two seniors who loved the game of lacrosse and wanted to share that love with their school. Now, it is a thriving community of players, coaches, and parents who all want nothing more than to have fun playing the creator's game. Our team is open to players of all levels of experience! This season, we are led by program manager Holly Bienawski and head coach Calvin Tsao and hope to carry our core values of effort, attitude, and toughness (EAT) both on and off the field every day.
Go Lynx!



ABOUT LACROSSE

The sport of lacrosse has its origins in Indigenous North America. It played a spiritual, ceremonial, and diplomatic role for a variety of Indigenous American nations in what is now Canada and the US. Today, it has been modified into the worldwide collegiate and professional sport you might recognize. Women's and men's lacrosse differ in rulesets and gear, but the ultimate objective is the same: using your stick, score more points than the other team by shooting the ball into their goal.
